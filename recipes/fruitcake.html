Fruit Cake

Description of the Meal

Making ample use of fruits found all over Hyrule, this cake is a must for celebrations.

Health recovery and other effects vary based on ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Wildberry or Apple
  • Any other Fruit
  • Cane Sugar
  • Tabantha Wheat

Ingredient Location

  1. Wildberry appear in colder regions
  2. Apples are found on trees in majority of Hyrule
  3. Cane sugar can be bought in various stores
  4. Tabantha Wheat can be bought or be collected in fields
