Pumpkin Pie

Description of the Meal

The intense sweetness of pumpkins makes this dessert popular among children.

Health recovery and other effects vary based on the pumpkin used.

Ingredients

  • Fortified Pumpkin or Sun Pumpkin
  • Cane Sugar
  • Goat Butter
  • Tabantha Wheat

Ingredient Location

  1. Fortified Pumpkin appear in rocky climates or caves
  2. Sun Pumpkin Appear in the high skys above hyrule
  3. Cane sugar can be bought in various stores
  4. Tabantha Wheat can be bought or be collected in fields
